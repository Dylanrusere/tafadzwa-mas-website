About Us

We began our journey in the events industry five years ago, initially specializing in weddings. Over the years, we’ve had the privilege of collaborating with talented students...

from prestigious institutions like Aleit. This experience has allowed us to grow and expand our expertise, infusing creativity and passion into every event we curate.

Towela Creatives is a dynamic and innovative event management company based in Zimbabwe, founded with a mission to transform the local event, PR, and hospitality industries. What began as a wedding and social event planning service has now evolved into a full-service agency, offering a broad range of services, including event planning and coordination, public relations, brand activations, and specialized event planning education.

Our journey reflects a deep commitment to not only providing exceptional services but also empowering aspiring event planners through our Towela Academy. Our vision is to become Zimbabwe’s leading integrated events and hospitality company, recognized for excellence in event management, public relations, and educational programs.
